How it works


You'll be presented with two choices. Choose which option is "worth more".

These choices could be a country, company, billionaire, cryptocurrency, or man-made object/project. Thus you will be comparing annual GDP, market cap valuations, net worth, and cost to make. In the context of money, it boils down to the question "Which would win?". Every correct answers adds to your score, but one wrong guess and the game ends. Good Luck!
